What We Do


Learn

Learn the basics of cardistry, card manipulation, and card magic.

If you've ever seen an effect and thought "I want to do that", now's your chance to learn! We'll start off with the very basics, but with the right amount of practice and dedication, you will be able to accomplish feats that most people would call magic.

Create

Design new tricks and effects by discussing card magic with fellow aficionados.

While learning techniques is always interesting, creating a new technique is something entirely different. If you've ever had an idea for a new technique, this is the place where you can meet with other technicians to turn your ideas into devastating illusions.

Impress

Perform on streets to bring the wonder of card magic to your audience.

What good is it if you have 1000 techniques, but no one to show them to? The point of magic and cardistry is to bring a sense of wonder into the lives of others, so take some time to go out and perform!


About Us


Once upon a time, in a land far far away, there lived five magicians, with powers beyond comprehension.


While we'd like to believe that's where our club originated, it isn't.

CSM is simply a club for people interested in either learning or practicing card manipulation. For people who are new to these topics, CSM offers a place to learn the basics of card magic, cardistry, and probability. For those who already have a background in the magical arts, CSM provides a place where practitioners can gather to create new effects or improve old ones.
